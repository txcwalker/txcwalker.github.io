Data was collected from Yelp using its API and a model was created to try and predict the average cost of a house by zip code. March Madness Region Strength

Region Strength Ideally we want our potential champion to come from the path of least resistance. That is to say the less competitive games our champion can play the more likely they are to win the championship. In an effort to quantify this path of least resistance (at least to the final four) the metric Region strength was created. It is comprised of three components, Total Region Championships, Seed Prediction and Fragility.
